An RxJS Subject is a special type of Observable that allows values to be multicasted to many Observers. While plain Observables are unicast, Subjects are multicast

(Unicast: Means each subscribed observer owns an independent execution of the observable. Multicast: Means one observable execution is shared among mulitple subscribers)
